Amanda Reis

Amanda Reis is another champion from SLS and on my opinion the best woman street-skater out there.


Amanda Reis (birth name – Amanda Kelly Reis) is a Brazilian Instagram celebrity and professional skateboarder, who is a 5-time gold medalist at the X Games as well as a 4-time winner of the World Cup Female Street Skate Championship.
